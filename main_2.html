Creative Ideas / 創作理念

喜愛自由的意志，讓我想設計出一種自由概念的標誌。主要標誌是以“人”的形象來設計，人張開手和腳象征愛好自由。為了符合各角度死角的問題，所以標誌倒轉后會形成藍天的鳥和夕陽的山

About motivation of freedom to design a free Logo. Logo is use the image of "man" to design, the man open his hands and legs is the mean freedom. In order to the blind angle problem, I design that the logo reverse can show the blue sky and mountain of sunset
